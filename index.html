About

Caio Ribeiro Pereira works as Web Developer at Concrete Solutions, he is from Brazil and his experience is based on understanding this alphabet soup: Node.js, Javascript, Meteor, Ruby, Rails, MongoDB, Redis, Riak, Linux, Lean Philosophy, Agile, XP and TDD.
Bachelor in Information Systems, blogger on his free time, admirer of programming, web, technology, movies and tv series. Actively engaged in the communities: NodeBr, DevInSantos and Meteor Brazil. Started as lecturer in 2011 at DevInSantos and Exatec events, approaching nowaday about Node.js and JavaScript. Creator of Node Web Modules, DevFreeCasts and founder of the Meteor Brazil community.
